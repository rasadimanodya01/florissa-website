About Florissa

A small business built on passion, creativity, and the love of handmade beauty.

How It Started

From a Hobby to a Business

Florissa started as a small hobby — making ribbon flowers for family and friends. What began as a creative outlet quickly grew into something much bigger when people started falling in love with the unique, handcrafted designs.

Every product at Florissa is made by hand using ribbon, pipe cleaners, and a whole lot of love. No machines, no mass production — just pure craftsmanship and creativity.

We believe that handmade gifts carry a special meaning. They show that someone took the time and care to create something truly unique — and that's exactly what Florissa is all about.
